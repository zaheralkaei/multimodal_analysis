Methodology & caveats

  • Visual analysis uses gemini-3-flash-preview via Ollama cloud. The model "sees" one mid-frame per shot and answers 8 questions. Quality depends on the chosen mid-frame.
  • Shot detection uses PySceneDetect's ContentDetector (HSV color delta + edge detection). Detects both hard cuts and gradual transitions. False positives possible in compression artifacts.
  • Camera motion is computed via OpenCV optical flow between consecutive frames within each shot (at 2 fps → 0.5s time resolution). Coarser than a human labeler but consistent. At lower fps, the optical flow algorithm tends to over-classify zoom-in because frames 1s+ apart often have apparent radial divergence.
  • Transcription uses faster-whisper. Trained on speech, not music. On heavily reverbed or whispered vocals, expect gaps or mistakes.
  • CLAP similarity: 0-1 probability per tag. High score = audio is similar to the tag, not that it is the tag.
  • "Cut on beat": shot start is within ±100ms of a detected beat. 47.2%% for this video; compare across videos for genre-level patterns.
  • Key detection uses Krumhansl-Schmuckler template matching on chroma. Works for most popular music; fails on atonal tracks.
